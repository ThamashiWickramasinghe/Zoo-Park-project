Our Shops

Bring the Zoo home with you! Explore a large collection of clothing, jewelry, souvenirs, books, toys and merchandise at several locations throughout the park.Gift shops are located inside the Visitor Center, at Panda Plaza, inside the Bird House and near the Great Cats exhibit. Outdoor gift items can also be found throughout the park.

Zoo Parc Pandas Shop

Discover our Zoo Parc Pandas shop, dedicated to the world of our giant pandas, at the park's entrance. You will love this area decorated in Chinese colours and maybe you will be tempted by our various panda collections, soft toys, tableware, decorative objects, clothing, medallions etc.

Uluwatu shop

To extend your journey to the heart of the equator, visit the Uluwatu shop, located in cafe area. A change of scenery is all but guaranteed thanks to products representing all the emblematic species that live in the squirrel monkeys, giant otters, Komodo dragons etc. Here, you will find our numerous residents displayed on a variety of products tableware, children/adult clothing, decorative products, stationery, soft toys etc. A bookshop area also offers around a hundred books dedicated to animals and nature.

Magic Moments shop

Also located at the South entrance to the park is our shop, designed to continue your journey to the planets furthest reaches. Here, you will find your favourite animals koalas, monkeys, big cats, tortoises, elephants in the form of soft toys, tableware, stationery, clothing, decoration, books, medallions etc. And if you are taking your time inside our shop, your friends and family will be able to wait on the brand-new shaded terrace. Benches, touch-screen terminals, and childrens games are all at your disposal.
