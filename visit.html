Plan To Visit

Enjoy the Zoo's amazing animals, nationally-recognized events, and award-winning educational programs. Make the most of your visit by planning ahead. From operating hours to purchasing tickets to learning about special exhibits and events, here's what you need to know to have a WILD day at Parc Zoo.

You can visit us all year round and have great and memorable experiences close to the animals. Our Zoo offers a wide range of activities that can be enjoyed by both children and the young at heart.

Note to Visitors

Exhibits are open, and there are dining and shopping experiences to enjoy and restrooms available for your convenience.


Getting To and Around the Zoo

All Zoo exhibits are wheelchair accessible. However, please be aware that the Zoo is located on zoo entrance. In the Visitor Center lobby, a lift gives wheelchair users access to restrooms and water fountains. Benches are located throughout the Zoo.

Dining

The Zoo Parc Zoo has fresh dining options available for guests. Stop by the Sea Lion Store and Cafe, located in our Sea Lion Court, featuring freshly prepared sandwiches, salads, and lots of healthy snacks. You can also bring a lunch and eat at one of our outdoor picnic tables.
