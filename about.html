About The Zoo

Zoo Parc is one of the oldest zoological park in the country.First opening on its current site in 1950.In the 1975s,The zoo parc Conservation Society assumed of the park under a contract and completed a dramatic five year transformasion.Since reopening in 1980.

The zoo is open every day of the year. From tropical birds, to snow leopards, grizzly bears, and one of the nation's largest colonies of Antarctic penguins, there are animals to enjoy in every season. The Wildlife Conservation Society's network of five urban wildlife parks headquartered at zoo parc have inspired and educated more than 400 million guests since first opening.
