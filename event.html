Events & Programs

To participate in the various programs held in our park you must get the membership of the park. There you can get information about the program.
Please register and Join this events/programs.

Login

The Beauval sea lion show

Come see and cheer for the graceful sea lions. within the beautiful swimming amphitheatre. These fantastic acrobats compete in games of skill and balance during the show! A wonderful chance to teach your family environmental conservation.

Dragons at Dusk Family Night

Families can journey to a world of pure imagination and enjoy an evening at the Zoo. Your ticket includes access to the entire Zoo and unlimited access to the Dragons & Mythical Creatures walk-through experience featuring nearly 60 life-size animatronic creatures that will ignite your sense of wonder!

Breakfast with the Elephants

In this special event, which takes place before to the Zoo opening, rise and shine among the herd of African elephants. After enjoying a wonderful breakfast can to join the zookeepers who look after the elephants to learn about their training, health, and other parts of their care.

Breakfast with the Giant Pandass

Discover the Faces of the Rainforest like never before by travelling to the tropics! Following an excellent breakfast buffet at thr zoo. While you join the giant pandas and their keepers for a training session behind the scenes, you can communicate with the professionals that look after the animals.

Brew at the Zoo

Experience your wild side at the Zoo, an adult-only nighttime event. Hundreds of beers, hard seltzers, and ready-to-drink cocktails , regional, and national beers will be available .Experience live music and meet animals, and more at the biggest outdoor beer festival .

Breakfast with the Zebra & Wildebeest

Enjoy a hot breakfast with animals to start your morning. As you assist zookeepers in setting up our mixed-species yard for zebras, wildebeest and watusi cattle, you will go 'on-exhibit' there!

Yoga with the Elephants

Take a seat next to our famous African elephants for a 60-minute yoga class while you get your mat and fill your reusable water bottle. The session is suitable for ALL fitness levels, will lead you through positions meant to strengthen your body and relax your mind.

Conservation Classroom

These live, 45-minute programs include pre- and post-activities that connect students to animals, conservation, and careers.
