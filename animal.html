The Pandas Are Coming

Giant pandas are returning ! As part of our 52-year-old conservation partnership with the China Wildlife Conservation Association, we are getting ready to welcome two new bears Bao Li and Qing Bao by the end of 2024.

In order to preserve giant pandas at the Zoo and save both this amazing species of bear and the mountain forest environments in which it lives, we need to fund $25 million. The rest of the zoo's operating budget, which supports the giant panda conservation program among other things, is funded by donations, members, and visitors, with federal funds covering the necessary needs for all the animals under our care.

CARING FOR ANIMALS

Ensuring Animal Wellness

Our goal is to save species, and the health of the creatures under our care is the first step towards that goal. Not only is it our duty, but it's also our passion.The passage continues. A young giraffe, an adult gorilla, and an elderly penguin require distinct environments in order to flourish. We will do all it takes to make sure they succeed. Experts in veterinary medicine, nutrition, husbandry, and display design collaborate to provide each animal, regardless of species, with personalised care throughout their lives.
