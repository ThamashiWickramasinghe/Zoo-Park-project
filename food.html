Food & Drink

There are more food areas to choose from at the ZooParc. Self-service, creperie, fast food and more outlets. Not all food outlets are open every day. When you get to the ZooParc, check our mobile site, our application.

Wood Cafe

Wood Cafe featuring offers delicious, made to order sandwiches and wraps, fresh salads, hot breakfast sandwiches and coffee. Also includes sweet food,ice cream. fruit salad and etc... Vegetarian options available.


Hours: Every Days at 10.00 am to 6.00pm

Burger Boss

Guests can choose from tasty favorites like 100% organic grass fed beef burgers, chicken sandwiches, vegetarian and vegan burgers, hot dogs, chicken tenders, fries and milkshakes made from hand-scooped ice cream. Indoor seating available.

Hours: Opens at 10:30 a.m.

Flame Pizza Hut

Enjoy quick bites and combo meals of hot pizza , chicken tenders, fresh salads and fries at this outdoor lunch stand with spacious courtyard seating. Vegetarian options available.


Hours: Opens at 9 a.m.

TUSKERS Cafe

Located in pandas area, Tuskers Cafe is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a variety of gourmet burgers, chicken sandwiches, and other classic menu options.If your choice vegetarians then you can bring vegi meals.

Hours: Opens at 10:00 a.m.

Food Truck Friday

End your week at Food Truck Friday an outdoor food truck festival at Zoo Parc starting at 5 pm. Attendance is free, just pay for what you eat and drink from an ever changing rotation of more than 15 food trucks each week.


Hours: Opens at 5:00p.m to 8:30p.m
